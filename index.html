What This Dashboard Does

The dashboard combines major sovereign yields, yield curve spreads, real yields, recession-style indicators and liquidity-sensitive variables into a single market regime framework.

Why It Matters

Bond markets often signal shifts in macro conditions before they become obvious elsewhere. This dashboard is designed to compress those signals into a readable market structure view.

Methodology

The Global Bond Market Dashboard combines simplified bond-market indicators into an internal BondStats regime classification model.

Inputs include sovereign yields, yield curve spreads, inflation-adjusted yields, recession-style indicators, liquidity stress assumptions and Basel-related demand support.

This BondStats dashboard is provided for informational and educational purposes only. It is a simplified analytical framework and does not represent investment advice, regulatory analysis or an official economic forecast.
